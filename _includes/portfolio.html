  • tiy - where art thou (final project)


    where.art.thou isn't just your typical online marketplace for buying and selling artwork, because where.art.thou is all about the WHERE. The application offers users the unique experience of purchasing art and supporting artists in their local community. Users can view art by location, filter by preference, save their favorite pieces, contact artists, and purchase artwork with payment processing via Stripe.


    TECHNOLOGIES: ruby on rails, json api, react, bootstrap

  • the iron library

    The Iron Library was an on-going project during my tenure at The Iron Yard Houston. This rails app 'scrapes' books from Amazon using the ruby mechanize gem, creates inventory in a postgreSQL database, and allows users to purchase books via Stripe.


    TECHNOLOGIES: material design lite (mdl), ruby on rails, json api, react
